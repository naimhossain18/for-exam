Recent news

The Bangabandhu Satellite-1 (Bangabandhu-1) is the first Bangladeshi geostationary communications and broadcasting satellite. It is named after the first president of Bangladesh, Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujibur Rahman. It was built by Thales Alenia Space and launched on a Falcon 9 rocket by SpaceX. The satellite provides telecommunications coverage to Bangladesh and neighboring countries. The satellite is controlled from ground stations in Gazipur and Betbunia, Rangamati.
